<p><p>Manual waveguide switches are devices used in microwave communication systems to direct the flow of electromagnetic waves. Rectangular waveguide switches are commonly used in applications where high power handling capabilities are required. They have a rectangular cross-section and offer low insertion loss. On the other hand, double ridge waveguide switches are designed for use in applications that require high frequency and low insertion loss. They have a double ridge structure that allows for better signal isolation and improved performance at higher frequencies.</p></p>
